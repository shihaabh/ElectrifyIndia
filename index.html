Our Solar Farms

Our world-class Solar farms create a sustainable ecosystem renewing energy and keeping transportation alive. Our solar panels are low-profile and durable — quietly converting sunlight to energy for decades to come. Integrated hardware and simple design achieve this by securing the panels close to your roof and to each other for a minimalist aesthetic. Superchargers can add up to 200 miles of range in just 15 minutes. Since charging above 80 percent is rarely necessary, stops are typically short and convenient. With a broad network of fast charging, automatic battery preconditioning and the exceptional range of every car, you’ll spend even more time on the road.
